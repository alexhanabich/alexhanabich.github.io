About me

I'm a software developer originally from South Korea, but now living in Utah, United States. I am a student in Brigham Young University majoring in Computer Science with an emphasis in Software Engineering.

I am passionate about learning new technologies, frameworks or languages and I enjoy working on small personal projects to create things that I would like to use.

Experiences

Research Student at DragnAI Lab (dragon.ai)

The first project was to help explore different domains for a model that allowed gamers to communicate with the AI team members with natural language. I created sereveral test inputs for domains such as smart house control, navigation, etc and ran the tests to see if the model would perform well in domains other than games.

Another project that I worked on was to POC(proof of concept) a better way to evaluate Conversational AI. The evaluation model would give a numerical score based on whether or not it is on topic, how polit it is, how interesting it is, if it is consistent, and if its using the correct grammar.
